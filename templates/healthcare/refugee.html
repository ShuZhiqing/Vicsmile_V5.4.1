Refugees and asylum seekers

Access to public dental care


If you are a refugee or asylum seeker you are entitled to public dental care in Victoria. You are also entitled to Medicare. Look up the link for advantage and benefit you can claim from the government.

Your local community dental clinic may sometimes cannot provide you the required service. In this situation, you may be given a voucher so that you can receive treatment from a private dentist that is valid for one to three months.

Call or visit the nearest dental clinic to you for question and appointment. If you do not speak English language very well, Translation and Interpreting Service may be useful for you.

Fee schedule

Free of charge. There is nothing you have to pay.

Waiting list

The general waiting list is not applied to you. You are entitled to priority access to dental care. You will be offered the next available appointment for general and denture care needs.
